## **Semester III - VIII Summary**  
Due to the document's length, only key highlights are presented below:
- **Semester III:** Focuses on Animation, Operating Systems, Data Structures, and System Design.
- **Semester IV:** Covers Software Engineering, Discrete Structures, DBMS, and Digital Electronics.
- **Semester V:** Includes Theory of Automata, Computer Networks, Compiler Design, Machine Learning, and Electives.
- **Semester VI:** Topics include Cloud Computing, Algorithm Design, Computer Organization, and Security.
- **Semester VII:** Advanced subjects like IoT, Soft Computing, Big Data Analytics, and a Minor Project.
- **Semester VIII:** Primarily focused on a Major Project, Internship, and Seminar.

**Total Credits Required for Graduation: 196**

## **Elective Courses**  
A variety of electives are available, including but not limited to:
- **IT Forensics, Advanced Communication Systems, Bioinformatics, Data Compression, Android Development, Distributed Databases, Robotics, and Data Mining.**

### **Additional Requirements**  
- **Industrial Training:** Minimum of 4 weeks after the third year.
- **Minor and Major Projects:** Evaluated as per university examination rules.
- **Internship in Industry:** Minimum of 4-6 months in the eighth semester.
